A word from our developers!

This webpage took a lot of contemplations, and lot of repeated attempts to get to an idea that we liked. Once the project started to take form we came all the way from a weather site that was supposed to be more about education and historical data all the way to a handy and creative travel site. As far as the team has noticed, there aren't many sites that can use an api such as this to make any trip you plan be so stress free! This site allows you to even check weather in advance of your trip, even gives you hotspots is the place you are planning to visit for your trip planning convinience!
